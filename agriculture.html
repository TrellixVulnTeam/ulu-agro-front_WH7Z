INNOVATION IN AGRICULTURE

Despite its difficult appearance, the agricultural sector is a place where great innovations occur every year. And it should be noted we are part of that process. Together with our partners at Timesoft LLC, we have developed a unique and modern intelligent management system specifically designed for agro-parks and growing enterprises. The main functions of the system are:

• Fully personalized profiles and access points depending on the responsibilities of employees;

• Take preventive measures based on data from satellite and Davis devices installed in the area;

• Ability to put together operations, management decisions, mapping, structures, labeling and welding costs;

• Availability of complete information for plants and land plots, including timelines and treatments;

• Special planning to manage and organize all management and operational activities within the organization;

• Monitoring the operation of equipment,facilities, machinery and security systems.
