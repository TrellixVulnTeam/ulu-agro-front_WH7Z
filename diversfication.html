DIVERSIFICATION

Growth is a fact of life, a necessity to grow stronger and adapt to a constantly changing work environment. We have also developed several projects related to our core activities to support rapid growth.

• Timesoft LLC: Founded for the development and integration of IT solutions, the tasks include the establishment of ERP or CRM systems, improved forms of other database function systems. Timesoft LLC's skilled team has the ability to manage any type of project based on the terms and plans of customer.

• Madena LLC: is a trading company that carries out all our export activities and organizes shipments to foreign customers. We already have Russia and Ukraine on our export map, and we plan to expand it every year.

• Multimoverz LLC: A transport and logistics company established to meet our delivery and export needs. It offers all kinds of logistics services, including air freight, rail, truck and container transportation, and even project shipments are within the authority of the company's skilled team. Our trading company organizes the sale of our products under the ULU CARDENS brand in the Russian and Ukrainian markets. Being a stable and reliable supplier and partner for our customers allows us to export products of the same brand to other farms. Negotiations are constantly underway to expand the geography of exports. Our experienced team participates in the largest exhibitions in the world, manages to increase their experience and contacts. We can mention Fruit Logistica Berlin, Sial Paris, World Food Moscow, World Food Dubai, Anuga China exhibitions as examples.
